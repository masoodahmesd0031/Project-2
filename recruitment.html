Our Recruitment Process

  • Client Consultation:
    • Understand client's hiring needs, organizational culture, and strategic objectives.
    • Define job requirements, qualifications, and desired attributes.
    • Establish clear communication channels and expectations.
  • Job Analysis and Position Description:
    • Conduct detailed job analysis to determine responsibilities, duties, and requirements.
    • Create comprehensive position description outlining key details.
  • Candidate Sourcing:
    • Utilize various methods (job boards, social media, networking) to identify potential candidates.
    • Implement targeted strategies to reach passive candidates.
    • Leverage industry-specific resources and networks for specialized talent.
  • Resume Screening and Evaluation:
    • Review resumes to identify qualified candidates.
    • Conduct initial screening interviews to assess skills and fit.
    • Use assessment tools to evaluate competencies.
  • Candidate Presentation to Client:
    • Present shortlisted candidates with detailed profiles, resumes, and assessment results.
    • Provide insights and recommendations based on candidate assessments.
    • Coordinate interview scheduling and logistics.
  • Interview Coordination and Feedback:
    • Facilitate interview sessions between client and candidates.
    • Gather feedback from both parties after each interview round.
    • Provide constructive feedback to candidates and address any concerns.
  • Offer Negotiation and Acceptance:
    • Aid in offer preparation, covering salary negotiations, benefits, and employment terms.
    • Act as intermediaries between client and candidates, facilitating communication and addressing concerns.
    • Ensure smooth and timely acceptance once an offer is extended and accepted.
  • Onboarding Support:
    • Ensure a seamless transition for both client and candidate into the new role.
    • Provide guidance on pre-employment requirements and orientation processes.
    • Follow up to address any issues or concerns.
  • Post-Placement Follow-Up:
    • Conduct follow-up with client and candidate for satisfaction with the process and outcome.
    • Remain available for ongoing support and to address additional needs or concerns.
